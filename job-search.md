# Job Search Agent — Instructions

## Step 1: Search these portals (in order)
1. https://www.naukri.com
2. https://www.linkedin.com/jobs
3. https://www.instahyre.com
4. https://wellfound.com/jobs (startup roles)
5. https://internshala.com (if entry-level roles found)

## Step 2: Search queries to run on each portal
Run ALL of these searches one by one:
- "Shopify Developer" Delhi NCR
- "Shopify Theme Developer" Delhi NCR
- "AI Automation Developer" Delhi NCR
- "AI Workflow Specialist" Delhi NCR
- "Frontend Developer React Next.js" Delhi NCR
- "Ecommerce Developer" Delhi NCR
- "Implementation Engineer" Delhi NCR
- Filter: Posted in last 7 days, Full-time + Remote/Hybrid

## Step 3: Evaluate each job listing
For every job found, score it using this rubric:

**A — Strong match (apply immediately)**
- Mentions Shopify OR AI automation/workflow OR Next.js/React
- Location: Delhi NCR or Remote/Hybrid
- Experience required: 0–2 years
- No mandatory DSA/CS degree requirement

**B — Good match (apply with tailored CV)**
- Overlaps 60–70% with my skills
- Minor gaps that can be addressed in cover note
- Location or experience slightly off but workable

**C — Weak match (skip)**
- Requires 3+ years OR DSA rounds OR ML engineering
- Outside Delhi NCR with no remote option
- No overlap with Shopify/AI/frontend skills

Only process A and B rated jobs. Skip all C.

## Step 4: For each A/B job — generate tailored CV
- Open cv.md
- Reorder skills section to match the JD's keywords
- Rewrite the Summary (2–3 lines) to reflect the exact role title
- Highlight the most relevant project(s) first
- Keep it to 1 page
- Save as: CV_[CompanyName]_[RoleTitle]_[Date].md

## Step 5: Apply
- Fill the application form using data from profile.md
- Upload the tailored CV
- For LinkedIn Easy Apply — use the built-in form fill
- For Naukri — use the Quick Apply or full form
- For other portals — navigate and fill manually

## Step 6: Log every application
Append to applications.md in this format:

| Date | Company | Role | Portal | Score | Status | CV Used |
|------|---------|------|--------|-------|--------|---------|
| YYYY-MM-DD | Company Name | Role Title | Naukri/LinkedIn | A/B | Applied/Pending | CV filename |

## Daily target
- Minimum 5 applications per session
- Prioritise A-rated jobs first, then B
- Do not apply to more than 2 C-rated jobs even if quota not met
